How to learn and speak Ukrainian fluently

I gues this article lends itself to the question - why learn Ukrainian? Is it because of teh Russian war? And to that answer I say no. My journey to this slavic langauge actually initiall started as what was an interest in the culture and its people after visiting Ukraine for the first time in September 2022, as a medical student. Yes you heard that right - a medical student in one of Ukraine's oldes universities in a small town known as Dnipropetskov or Dnipro for short.

Sadly, things didn't work out and currently I am lost again, asking myself what is my purpose? What is my calling?

Unfortunately I don't have the answer to this yet however one thing I am certain of is that my journey forged some strange kind of connection with this country and its people and, simply put, I can't stop thinking about the language and how different their culture and cuisine is from my "norm" - so, I ventured out and took on the challenge of learning Ukrainian, and this page will serve as my platform to share my journey.

I also really want to learn Ukrainian so I can get my hands on some medical journals written by historically renowned physicians such as Nikolai Ivanovich Pirogov - Russia's titan of anatomy in the 19th century.
